Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian comfort food made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. It’s a hearty and satisfying dish that’s perfect for family dinners or special occasions. This version of lasagna combines flavorful ground beef, tomato sauce, and perfectly cooked lasagna noodles, all baked together to create a golden, bubbling masterpiece. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned cook, this recipe is sure to impress!

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • Tomato paste (2 tablespoons)
  • Olive oil (2 tablespoons)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles.
  3. Prepare the meat sauce.
  4. Mix the ricotta cheese with egg.
  5. Layer the ingredients in a baking dish.
